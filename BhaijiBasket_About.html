BhaijiBasket is a simple, easy to use app designed for you to shop with ease and comfort.

We are three aspiring engineers and we wanted to come up with a product
that contributes to the simplicity of society. So we have designed BhaijiBasket, tailored for your comfort.

Shop food from restaurants, fast food chains, or even groceries from stores, by simply sitting in a chair and adding items to a cart. Yes, it is that easy.
